About Us

Toner Warehouse inc. supplies and services Waterloo region's businesses with all their toner and office supply needs. Specializing in laser cartridges, we have been offering top quality products since 2000.

We sell new and remanufactured toner cartridges of top brands including: Brother, Canon, Dell, Epson, Hp, Konica Minolta, Lexmark, OKI, Panasonic, Ricoh, Samsung, Sharp, Xerox and more!

We also retail all types of fax machines, copiers and printers. Our certified technician is available on-site and with years of experience to service all types of office machines.
